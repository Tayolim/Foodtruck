OUR STORY

The Lonely Traveler started off as a dream of cooking food for people in the kitchen. Our founder, Tasha, constantly joked about running a food truck as a retirement plan during her college years until her roommates suggested she doesn't wait for retirement to do something she loves. Tasha decided to go on a gap year after college and finally start the food truck she she's been talking about all those years. The rest was history.

We started in B-Town's food truck friday, and then expanded our traveling to neighboring states. As we started growing our fan base for our famous karaage wrap and secret special sauce, we decided that we would go back to our roots and open up a pop up shop in Bloomington.

We'll never be tied down though. We're going to one day go global and travel the world!

In the mean time, feel free to play our game and follow us on our journey through our social media.
